what is it? ✨

Motion graphics is where design comes to life. This space showcases a collection of short, looped animations that explore movement, rhythm, and visual storytelling.

Each piece focuses on transforming static visuals into engaging, dynamic moments — whether through subtle transitions, experimental effects, or bold kinetic compositions. These works highlight my approach to timing, pacing, and how motion can enhance both concept and aesthetic.
