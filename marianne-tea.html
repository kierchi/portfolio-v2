the brief ✦

Marianne's Tea is a heritage English tea brand with a history of being served to royalty. The brand carries that legacy forward — classic, quality-driven, and deeply rooted in tradition — while stepping into a more modern, refined aesthetic.

The visual identity draws from blue and white porcelain: the striking contrast, the intricate floral patterns, and the quiet sense of elegance that porcelain carries. Everything in the brand grew from that one reference point.

Each variety in the Marianne's range is given its own colour world — Sakura Blend in soft cherry blossom pinks, Jasmine Green in watercolour sage — while the shared typography and logo mark keep the collection unified. The tea sachet carries the same identity down to the smallest touchpoint, ensuring the brand experience is consistent from shelf to cup.
