the brief ✦

Teeny Me is a dress-up game concept with fully customisable avatars — mix-and-match outfits, accessories, hairstyles, and expressions. The goal was to create a visual identity that feels playful, inclusive, and packed with personality.

Every design decision, from the UI buttons to the character proportions, was made to feel like it belonged in the game world itself.
