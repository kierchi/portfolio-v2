i'm kaila ♥

I'm a graphic designer based in Vancouver with a deep love for packaging design, motion graphics, and video editing. My design sensibility comes from years of drawing, consuming anime, and being genuinely obsessed with art that makes you feel something.

Whether I'm crafting a full brand identity, designing packaging that stands out on a shelf, or editing a reel that keeps people from swiping — I bring the same energy: thoughtful, intentional, and a little bit fun.

"From atmospheric to adorable — whatever the concept needs." ✦
